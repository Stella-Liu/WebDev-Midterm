Princess Ida (Silent Princess)


The player helps Princess Ida solve the puzzles. She is also known as Silent Princess, because never talks and the Thieving Princess who left Monument Valley after stealing the sacred geometry, which makes one forget their true selves. We help Princess Ida return the sacred geometry in each Chapter. At the end of the original game, the Princess and the crows remember their true selves and are set free.

Totem


We first meet the Totem in Chapter 6, The Labyrinth. Princess Ida's helper in parts of the game who we also play during certain chapter and scenes. At the end of Chapter 6, we assumed the Totem drowned trying to follow us, but he reappears in Chapter 9. So happy news, the Totem never died. He will be broken into parts, but he can be reassembled.

The StoryTeller


We meet her in Chapter 3, Hidden Temple. She tells the story of the game. When we first meet her, she will tell us about the sacred geometry and indicate that the crows are men who have stolen the sacred geometry and the punishment they recieved. Later, she asks Princess Ida, why she had returned which tells us the purpose of the sacred geometry.

A Crow


The first encounter with a crow is in Chapter 4, Water Palace. These crows squawks at you when you walk by. They block your path and are sometimes used to make the path for you to walk. All the crows are mens of Monument Valley who stole the sacred geometry have forgotten their true selves and forced to walk Monument Valley.
